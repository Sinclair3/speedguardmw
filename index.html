SpeedGuard Malawi — Terms of Use v2.0

1. Purpose. SpeedGuard Malawi is a road safety awareness tool for the M1 road between Lilongwe and Blantyre, Malawi.

2. Not an evasion tool. This app does NOT encourage speeding or evading law enforcement. You remain fully responsible for obeying all Malawi traffic laws.

3. Location & live tracking. Your nickname, approximate GPS location, and speed are shared in real time with other active users while the app is open with GPS on. This data is never stored permanently.

4. SOS feature. The emergency SOS button sends your GPS coordinates to a contact you choose. The accuracy of GPS coordinates depends on your device signal. This feature is a supplement to, not a replacement for, calling emergency services directly.

5. Community reports. Trap locations submitted by users may not be accurate. Always drive within legal limits regardless of reported trap positions.

6. Privacy. Only your nickname and approximate location are shared with other drivers. Your emergency contact number is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

7. Disclaimer. SpeedGuard is provided "as is." The developers are not liable for any fines, accidents, or losses. Never use your phone while driving.

8. Age & licence. You must be 18+ and hold a valid driving licence.

9. Updates. Terms may be updated at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

🔒 Privacy at a Glance
  • Nickname & live location visible to other drivers while GPS is on
  • Emergency contact stored on your device only — never uploaded
  • No personal data stored permanently on any server
  • Never interact with your phone while driving
⚠️ Safety Warning
For passenger use or pre-journey review only. Mount phone securely or have a passenger manage the app. Distracted driving is dangerous and illegal in Malawi.
