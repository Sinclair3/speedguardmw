SpeedGuard Malawi — Terms of Use v2.0

1. Purpose. SpeedGuard Malawi is a road safety awareness tool to help drivers on the M1 road stay informed about speed limits and reported speed enforcement zones between Lilongwe and Blantyre.

2. Not an evasion tool. This app does NOT encourage speeding or evading law enforcement. You remain fully responsible for obeying all Malawi traffic laws at all times.

3. Location & live tracking. By using this app you agree that your nickname, approximate GPS location, and speed will be visible to other active users in real time. This data is broadcast only while the app is open with GPS on, and is never stored permanently.

4. Community reports. Trap locations submitted by users are community-generated and may not be accurate. Always drive within legal speed limits regardless of reported trap locations.

5. Privacy. Only your chosen nickname and approximate location are shared. No personal details (full name, phone number, national ID) are collected or stored.

6. Disclaimer. SpeedGuard is provided "as is" without warranty. The developers are not liable for any fines, accidents, or losses from use of this app. Never use your phone while driving.

7. Age & licence. You must be 18 or older and hold a valid driving licence to use this app.

8. Malawi law. Use of this app must comply with all applicable laws of the Republic of Malawi, including the Road Traffic Act.

9. Updates. Terms may be updated at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the latest terms.

🔒 Privacy at a Glance
  • Your nickname & live location visible to other drivers while GPS is on
  • No personal data stored permanently anywhere
  • Community trap reports may not be 100% accurate
  • Never interact with your phone while driving
⚠️ Safety Warning
For passenger use or pre-journey review only. Mount your phone securely or have a passenger manage the app. Distracted driving is dangerous and illegal in Malawi.
